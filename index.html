Our Services

Matrimonial Law deals with all legal aspects of marriage, divorce, maintenance, child custody, domestic violence, and other family-related issues.

Property Law governs the ownership, use, and transfer of both movable and immovable property. It ensures legal clarity in property rights and resolves disputes related to real estate.

Company Law governs the formation, operation, regulation, and dissolution of companies. It ensures businesses function transparently, ethically, and within legal limits.

Intellectual Property (IP) Law protects creations of the mind—like inventions, brand names, artistic works, and designs—ensuring creators get recognition and control over their ideas.

Restructuring and Insolvency Law helps companies and creditors manage financial distress, revive struggling businesses, or ensure fair and legal exit when revival is not possible.
